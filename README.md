## ğŸ® How to Use

### Load a Video
1. Click **"Open Video File"** button
2. Select your video file (MP4, MOV, or WEBM)
3. Video loads automatically

### Playback Controls
- **Play/Pause Button** - Click or press `Spacebar`
- **Volume Slider** - Adjust audio level
- **Time Display** - Shows current time / total duration

### Timeline Navigation
- **Click anywhere** on the timeline to jump to that point
- **Drag the scrubber** (white circle) to smoothly navigate
- Video auto-pauses while dragging for precise control
